Mercedes Benz

Mercedes-Benz is a legendary brand of the German auto giant - Daimler AG. As a result of the insatiable search for innovations, Mercedes-Benz has become the world's first automobile brand, which stands for comfortable, durable and elegant cars. Today, a wide range of vehicles and purposes are produced under the Mercedes-Benz brand: passenger cars, passenger/military light SUVs, trucks, buses, minivans, etc.
